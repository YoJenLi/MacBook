Company Overview

company (NYSE: MXL), where the entrepreneurial spirit is alive and well. We
strive to hire the best people in the world as we look toward numerous
opportunities in our broadband markets.

radio-frequency (RF) analog and mixed-signal semiconductor solutions for
broadband communications applications. Based near San Diego in Carlsbad,
California, with design centers in Irvine, California, India and China, we
have nearly 300 employees, most with engineering degrees including master’s
and Ph.D. graduates from many of the premiere universities around the world.
