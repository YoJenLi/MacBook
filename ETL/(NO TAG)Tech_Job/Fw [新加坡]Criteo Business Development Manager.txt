【工作內容】
About the role
You will be responsible for media buying for Taiwan, Hong
Kong & South East Asia. This will include identifying premium
publishers, developing relationships, negotiating terms, and
managing and growing such partners. You will monitor and
optimize performance advertising campaigns, along with
cultivating strong and productive relationship with media
partners.

Responsibilities
- Identifying, pitching & signing premium publishers in Taiwan,
  Hong Kong & South East Asia
- Negotiating legal contracts - business terms and conditions
  with premium publishers
- Improving placement positions & inventory volume
- Implementing and trafficking of new online campaigns
- Contributing to building the Criteo service and brand
  awareness in the region


【徵求條件】
REQUIREMENTS
1. Strong analytical skills (Pivot Tables, Tableau)
2. Minimum 2-3 years of experience in Sales/Business Development/
   Marketing function in the digital field.
3. 4 year University degree or equivalent. MBA preferred.
4. Experience in publisher business development with good access
   to premium publishers - a strong plus.
5. Good understanding or a willingness to learn implementation of
   the Publisher ad serving technology (DFP, OpenX) preferred.
6. 需精通繁體中文之聽說讀寫及流暢的商用英語能力，若有與台灣客戶洽
   商的經驗者為佳。
